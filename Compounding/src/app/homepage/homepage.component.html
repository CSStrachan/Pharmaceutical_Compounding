Welcome to my Compoudning App


Welcome to our compounding application, an essential tool for pharmacy professionals and anyone interested in personalized medication. Our app offers a user-friendly interface that allows you to easily calculate the ratio of medication to filler for compounded prescriptions. You can choose the best filler for your patients based on their specific needs, including lactose allergies. Additionally, our app includes native Android camera functionality, making it easy to capture images of your compounded preparations for documentation and sharing. With our app, you can save time, streamline your workflow, and provide high-quality, personalized medication to your patients.
